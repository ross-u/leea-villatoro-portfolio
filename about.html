Hi, I'm Leea

I am a user experience designer and it’s my mission to create an intuitive and delightful user experience in every step of the user’s journey. I constantly challenge myself in my design work and I keep up-to-date with new skills and technologies in design.

In my past experience as a hospitality professional, I’ve learned that the best way to understand and empathize is through listening and research. I realized that User Experience Design is something I can truly enjoy as a long-life career because it combines my passions which are working with people and design. I strive to synergize my experience, knowledge and skills to create solutions that will have a positive impact on people's lives.

I am a New Yorker originally from Honduras, I was a sailor and I lived in a ship for 7 years. I love to travel and I have been to 45 countries. My favourite countries are Russia, Turkey and Norway.

In my free time, I enjoy going to the beach, shopping, listening to podcasts and I'm constantly learning more about how Design and Development play a huge role in creating future Designers.
